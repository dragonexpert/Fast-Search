Fast Search

Fast Search is designed to greatly improve the speed of your searches.  It does this by eliminating the memory eating processes
that the default search system uses.

Installation:
Upload search2.php to your forum root.
Upload /inc/plugins/fastsearch.php to your /inc/plugins folder.
Activate in the Admin CP

Upgrading:
Simply Replace the files of the old version with the new version.

Suggestions:
Please create an issue on Github.
